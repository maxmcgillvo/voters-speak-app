Our Mission

Connecting Citizens with Their Representatives

Voters Speak exists to bridge the gap between American citizens and their elected officials. We believe that democracy thrives when citizens can easily access and communicate with the people who represent them in government.

What We Do

Voters Speak is a comprehensive, non-partisan platform that provides:

  • Centralized Directory: All your elected officials in one convenient location
  • Complete Contact Information: Phone numbers, email addresses, and official websites
  • Social Media Links: Direct access to official social media accounts
  • All Branches of Government: Executive, Legislative (Senate & House), and Judicial
  • Always Up-to-Date: Regular updates to ensure accuracy of information
  • Free Access: No registration, no fees, no barriers to civic engagement

Our Values

  • Transparency: All information is publicly sourced and clearly presented
  • Non-Partisanship: We serve all citizens regardless of political affiliation
  • Accessibility: Free, open access for everyone
  • Accuracy: Commitment to providing correct, up-to-date information
  • Privacy: No tracking, no data collection, no user accounts required
  • Civic Responsibility: Encouraging respectful, constructive engagement

How to Use Voters Speak

Using our platform is simple:

  1. Choose a Branch: Select Executive, Senate, House, or Supreme Court
  2. Find Your Representative: Use search and filter tools to locate specific officials
  3. Get Contact Information: Access phone numbers, emails, and websites
  4. Reach Out: Contact your representatives to share your views
  5. Stay Informed: Follow official social media accounts for updates

Data Sources

All information on Voters Speak is obtained from official, publicly available sources:

  • Official U.S. Senate and House of Representatives websites
  • The White House official website
  • Supreme Court official website
  • Congressional directories and public records
  • Verified official social media accounts
  • Government public information databases

Commitment to Civic Engagement

We believe that an informed, engaged citizenry is essential to a healthy democracy. By making it easier to contact elected officials, we hope to:

  • Increase citizen participation in government
  • Strengthen the connection between representatives and constituents
  • Promote transparency and accountability
  • Encourage respectful political discourse
  • Support the democratic process

Future Development

We are continuously working to improve Voters Speak. Planned features include:
